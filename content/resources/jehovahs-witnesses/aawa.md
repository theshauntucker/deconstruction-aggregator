AAWA is a nonprofit advocacy organization founded by survivors and former members dedicated to exposing institutional abuse within Jehovah's Witnesses and pressuring the Watchtower for accountability. The organization maintains extensive documentation of cases, coordinates survivor testimony, and works to ensure that abuse allegations reach appropriate authorities and media.

The organization serves as both a resource hub and a coordinating body for survivor activism, maintaining a database of documented cases and coordinating testimony for legal proceedings and investigations. AAWA's work has directly contributed to government inquiries in multiple countries.
