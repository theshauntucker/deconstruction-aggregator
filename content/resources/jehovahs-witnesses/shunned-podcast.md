The Shunned Podcast is dedicated to examining one of the most damaging JW practices: mandatory disfellowshipping and shunning of family members and friends. Episodes explore the mechanics of this practice, its psychological impacts, and stories of family estrangement caused by JW doctrinal enforcement.

For those attempting to understand JW institutional harm, shunning is often the most immediate and painful manifestation of organizational control. This podcast provides both the human stories and the structural analysis of how JW policy turns family members into enforcers of institutional discipline.
