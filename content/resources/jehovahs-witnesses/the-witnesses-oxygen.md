Oxygen's *The Witnesses* is a four-part documentary series that brings investigative journalism to the JW abuse crisis, featuring survivor testimony, expert analysis, and the Watchtower's documented failures in child protection. The series captures both the human cost of institutional abuse and the systematic structures that enabled perpetrators to operate with impunity within congregations.

The documentary's strength is its accessibility and mainstream legitimacy—it introduces the abuse issue to audiences unfamiliar with JW practices, showing not only individual cases but the institutional patterns. Survivor interviews provide emotional weight, while documentary evidence reveals the bureaucratic mechanisms of cover-up and harm.
