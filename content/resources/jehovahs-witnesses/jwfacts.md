JWFacts is a long-standing online encyclopedia dedicated to examining Jehovah's Witnesses doctrine, history, and institutional practices. The site provides detailed articles on core JW beliefs, failed prophecies, organizational structure, and documented controversies, all supported by extensive citations from official Watchtower publications and independent sources.

The resource is particularly valuable for those seeking to understand the theological foundations of JW doctrine and how those beliefs translate into practical policies—such as disfellowshipping rules, blood transfusion prohibitions, and restrictions on higher education. Visitors can cross-reference claims with original Watchtower sources, making it an essential reference for comparative analysis.
