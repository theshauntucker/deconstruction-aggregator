JW.support is a community-driven resource site offering practical guidance for those leaving the JW organization. The site provides information on managing the psychological impact of deconversion, coping with shunning and family estrangement, rebuilding identity outside the religion, and accessing mental health resources tailored to JW exit experiences.

The site's value lies in its focus on concrete transition support rather than criticism alone. For newly departing members navigating the disorientation of leaving a high-control religion, JW.support offers accessible guidance on rebuilding social connections, addressing cult-induced trauma, and finding community outside the organization.
