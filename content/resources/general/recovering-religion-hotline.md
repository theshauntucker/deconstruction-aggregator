Recovering from Religion operates the 1-844-I-DOUBT-IT (1-844-843-2868) hotline, a crisis and support line specifically for people in crisis during religious deconstruction from any tradition. The hotline is staffed by trained volunteers who have themselves experienced religious trauma and deconstruction, providing peer support rooted in understanding of what callers are experiencing.

The hotline is invaluable for people experiencing suicidal ideation, severe depression, family crisis related to deconstruction, or overwhelming doubt and fear during the process of leaving religion. Unlike general mental health crisis lines, the hotline staff understand the specific impacts of religious trauma, the shame and fear that accompany deconstruction, and the isolation that religious leaving often entails. Callers can speak with someone who has navigated similar journeys.

For anyone in crisis, the Recovering from Religion hotline is the most important resource available. The organization also provides online support groups, local meetup groups in many areas, and additional resources for ongoing support beyond crisis intervention. Having access to trained peers who validate the reality of religious harm while supporting safety and healing can be literally life-saving for those in acute crisis.
