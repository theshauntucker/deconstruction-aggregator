The Conspirituality Podcast, hosted by Cerie Bullivant and Julian Robles, examines the convergence between conspiracy theories, spiritual and wellness communities, and new-age ideology. The podcast is essential for understanding how QAnon, anti-vaccination movements, and other conspiracy narratives have infiltrated spiritual spaces, transforming wellness communities into vehicles for radicalization and extremism.

The hosts trace how spiritual concepts like ascension, awakening, and enlightenment have been reinterpreted within conspiracy frameworks, and how wellness culture's emphasis on "trusting your own truth" creates openness to unverified conspiracy claims. The podcast documents specific influencers and communities that have promoted conspiracy theories while maintaining spiritual branding, revealing how spiritual authority and misinformation are weaponized together.

For those in or leaving spiritual, new-age, or wellness communities, the Conspirituality Podcast provides critical analysis of how these spaces have become radicalized. The show explains cult-like dynamics within wellness communities and why rejecting conspiracy theories can feel like spiritual betrayal to believers. The podcast serves as both educational resource and validation for those concerned about loved ones in conspiracy-influenced spiritual spaces.
