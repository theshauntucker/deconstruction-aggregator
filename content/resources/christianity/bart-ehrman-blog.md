The Bart Ehrman Blog provides ongoing scholarly commentary on New Testament scholarship, historical Jesus research, early Christian history, and biblical criticism. The blog is subscription-based with proceeds donated to charity, providing Ehrman a platform to share longer-form scholarship and engage with reader questions. For those who have read Ehrman's popular books and want deeper scholarly engagement with biblical criticism, the blog is an invaluable resource.

The blog covers topics including specific passages and their historical context, debates within New Testament scholarship, discoveries of ancient manuscripts and their significance, and responses to common Christian apologetic arguments. Ehrman's expertise and his accessibility make the blog useful for both advanced students of New Testament scholarship and general readers wanting to deepen their understanding of the historical Jesus and early Christianity. The interactive nature—with Ehrman responding to reader questions—creates ongoing engagement with these topics.

For someone who has encountered Ehrman's work and wants to continue learning from him as they deconstruct from Christianity, the blog provides a direct line to ongoing expert commentary on the historical and textual issues that most challenge fundamentalist Christian claims.
