Genetically Modified Skeptic is a well-produced YouTube channel by Drew, a former fundamentalist Christian who deconstructs religious apologetics and examines claims made by Christian apologists. The channel combines Drew's personal deconstruction narrative with systematic analysis of arguments used to defend Christianity, including responses to popular Christian apologists like William Lane Craig. Drew's approach is respectful but uncompromising in scrutinizing the logical and evidential foundations of faith claims.

The channel is particularly valuable for people in the early stages of faith deconstruction who are encountering apologetic arguments they haven't yet learned to evaluate critically. Drew models how to examine claims carefully, identify logical fallacies and unsupported assumptions, and recognize the difference between rhetorical persuasiveness and actual evidence. His videos cover topics including biblical reliability, the problem of evil, resurrection claims, and the historical Jesus. The production quality and clear explanations make the content accessible to someone newly questioning their faith, while the depth of analysis appeals to more advanced thinkers.

For evangelicals in particular, Genetically Modified Skeptic provides both intellectual scaffolding for evaluating faith claims and emotional reassurance that leaving Christianity based on intellectual honesty is not a failure or moral failing but a rational response to examining evidence.
