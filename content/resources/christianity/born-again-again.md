Born Again Again is a podcast platform for sharing deconstruction and reconstruction stories from people who have experienced faith shifts. The podcast features interviews with individuals at various stages of their faith journeys—some who have completely deconverted, others who have moved to progressive Christianity, and still others exploring their relationship with faith. The diversity of experiences and outcomes makes the podcast valuable for people uncertain about what their own deconstruction will look like or what options exist beyond fundamentalist Christianity.

The hosts approach these conversations with genuine curiosity rather than agenda, creating space for complexity in faith journeys. Episodes avoid the assumption that everyone who questions faith will eventually become atheist; instead, the podcast documents the range of outcomes—full deconstruction, progressive faith, spiritual but not religious identities, and other positions. For someone beginning to question their faith, Born Again Again provides multiple models for what deconstruction and reconstruction might look like.

The podcast is particularly useful for people who feel overwhelmed by more confrontational deconstruction spaces and need to see diverse, compassionate models of faith questioning that don't pressure toward any particular outcome.
