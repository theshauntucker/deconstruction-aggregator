The Cult Education Institute, founded by renowned cult intervention specialist Rick Ross, maintains one of the largest freely accessible archives of information about cults and high-control groups. The website contains thousands of articles, news reports, academic papers, survivor testimonies, and organizational profiles covering virtually every documented cult and high-control group from the 20th century to present.

Despite its disorganized presentation, the Cult Education Institute is invaluable for research, verification, and understanding specific groups. The site allows users to search by organization name, exploring comprehensive documentation of that group's history, documented abuse, survival stories, and exit resources. Ross's decades of cult intervention work are reflected in the carefully collected materials that provide both historical perspective and current information.

For someone investigating a specific group or seeking to understand whether their experience matches patterns documented for their organization, the Cult Education Institute provides the breadth of information necessary to contextualize individual experiences within broader cult dynamics. The site demonstrates that high-control groups share consistent patterns regardless of their surface ideology, providing validation for survivors who may feel their experiences are unique when they actually reflect universal cult mechanisms.
