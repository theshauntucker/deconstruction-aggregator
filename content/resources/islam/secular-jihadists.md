The Secular Jihadists Podcast features discussions with ex-Muslims, critics of Islam, and intellectuals examining Islamic theology, politics, and contemporary issues from secular and humanist perspectives. The podcast explores complex topics including theological contradictions, the historical Muhammad, Islamic law, women's rights within Islamic contexts, and the experiences of those leaving the faith. Hosted by knowledgeable guests and producers, the podcast combines educational content with personal storytelling.

Available on major podcast platforms, the show serves both as an entry point for those beginning to question Islam and as a deeper resource for more advanced deconstructors seeking intellectual engagement with theological and political critiques of Islamic doctrine and Islamic governance.
