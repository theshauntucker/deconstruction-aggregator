WikiIslam is a comprehensive, encyclopedia-style wiki dedicated to critical examination of Islamic theology, history, and contemporary practice. It compiles extensive source material, scholarly analysis, and user-contributed articles covering topics from Quranic contradictions and hadith reliability to Islamic history and modern Islamic states. The resource is meticulously sourced and well-organized, making it valuable for those seeking to understand criticisms of Islam from multiple angles.

Licensed under Creative Commons BY-NC 3.0, WikiIslam's content is freely accessible and can be shared for non-commercial purposes with proper attribution. The site serves as an entry point for intellectual criticism of Islamic doctrine and is particularly useful for researchers, students, and individuals deconstructing their faith.
