Bread and Roses TV is a YouTube channel focused on ex-Muslim activism with particular emphasis on women's rights, Iran, and the intersection of religious oppression with gender-based violence. The channel combines documentary-style content, interviews with activists, and analysis of women's experiences under Islamic law and governance. The work centers the voices of women who have experienced Islamic patriarchy and those fighting for secular, egalitarian alternatives.

The channel serves both as an educational resource on women's experiences in Islamic contexts and as a platform for ex-Muslim women activists. For those interested in understanding how Islamic doctrine and law specifically harm women, and how women-led activism shapes ex-Muslim movements, Bread and Roses TV provides vital perspective.
