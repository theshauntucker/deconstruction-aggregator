Abdullah Sameer is a popular ex-Muslim YouTuber who creates content exploring his journey away from Islam, examining Islamic theology and history, and providing support and community for others deconverting. His videos cover topics ranging from Quranic inconsistencies and hadith problems to personal reflections on family dynamics, identity, and the psychological process of leaving religion. Sameer's approachable style makes complex theological and historical critiques accessible to those new to deconstruction.

With a large following, Sameer's channel serves as both an educational resource and a validating community space for ex-Muslims at various stages of their journey. His willingness to engage with difficult questions and his transparency about his own ongoing process make him a trusted voice in the online ex-Muslim community.
