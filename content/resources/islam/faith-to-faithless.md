Faith to Faithless is a UK-based secular organization supporting individuals leaving any religion, including Islam. The organization provides peer support, counseling referrals, practical resources, and community spaces for people navigating religious deconstruction. While not exclusively focused on ex-Muslims, Faith to Faithless recognizes the particular challenges of leaving high-control religious environments and offers tailored support for individuals from fundamentalist or authoritarian faith backgrounds.

The organization combines individual support with public advocacy for secular values and religious freedom, working to destigmatize apostasy and defend the rights of those who leave faith. For UK residents leaving Islam seeking support within a secular framework, Faith to Faithless offers an accessible entry point to community and resources.
