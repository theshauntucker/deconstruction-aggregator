Footsteps is a New York City-based nonprofit organization providing comprehensive support for individuals leaving Orthodox Judaism. The organization offers educational programs, vocational training, mental health resources, and community building for OTD individuals navigating the transition from insular religious communities to secular life. Recognizing that those raised in ultra-Orthodox environments often lack basic secular skills and credentials, Footsteps provides practical pathways to economic independence and social integration.

The organization addresses the unique vulnerability of OTD individuals: many have been given minimal secular education, leaving them unprepared for employment; many face family estrangement and economic abandonment; many struggle with trauma, grief, and identity reconstruction. Footsteps combines practical support with community, creating a safe space for people rebuilding their lives outside Orthodox Judaism.
