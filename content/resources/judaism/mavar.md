Mavar is a UK-based organization providing support and resources for individuals leaving or who have left Orthodox Judaism. The organization addresses the specific challenges OTD individuals face in the UK context, including housing insecurity, employment barriers due to limited secular education, family estrangement, and psychological trauma. Mavar combines practical support with peer community, recognizing that religious transition is both a practical and emotional journey.

For UK residents leaving Orthodox Judaism, Mavar provides an accessible entry point to support and community resources. The organization's existence acknowledges that while OTD experiences share commonalities with other religious transitions, the unique culture and structure of Orthodox communities requires specialized support designed by and for those who have lived these experiences.
