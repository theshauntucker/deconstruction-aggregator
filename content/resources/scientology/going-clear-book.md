# Going Clear: Scientology and the Prison of Belief

Lawrence Wright's *Going Clear* is widely regarded as the most authoritative written investigation of Scientology. The Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist spent years researching the organization, interviewing defectors including David Miscavige's former assistant, and reviewing extensive court filings. The book traces Scientology's origins through L. Ron Hubbard's life, documenting the historical development of the organization's belief system and increasingly coercive practices.

Wright systematically examines the disconnection policy, the Sea Org, financial exploitation, and the Fair Game doctrine with meticulous sourcing. This is essential foundational reading for anyone seeking to understand Scientology's structure and methods of control.
