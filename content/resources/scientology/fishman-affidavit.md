# Fishman Affidavit / OT Materials

The Fishman Affidavit is a court filing that included Scientology's most closely-guarded upper-level teachings—the OT (Operating Thetan) materials. Though Scientology has claimed copyright, these materials are part of the public court record. The affidavit provides rare access to the esoteric cosmology and practices Scientology charges $300,000-500,000+ to reach.

Link to archival sources rather than hosting directly, given ongoing Scientology litigation over these materials. The OT materials reveal the organization's ultimate teachings about alien history, reincarnation, and spiritual exercises. Understanding these materials is crucial to comprehending Scientology's belief system and why the organization invests so heavily in their secrecy.
