# Blown for Good: Behind the Iron Curtain of Scientology

Marc Headley spent 27 years in Scientology's most restricted and controlled environment—the International Base in California where the highest-level operations and David Miscavige's personal headquarters are located. His memoir *Blown for Good* provides an insider account of conditions at the International Base, the structure of the Sea Org, daily work practices, and the psychological and physical isolation tactics used to maintain control.

Headley's detailed narrative covers his childhood in Scientology through his dramatic escape. The book includes specific documentation of work schedules, financial manipulation, and organizational hierarchies. Combined with his federal lawsuit, Headley's testimony constitutes one of the most comprehensive accounts of high-level organizational life within Scientology.
