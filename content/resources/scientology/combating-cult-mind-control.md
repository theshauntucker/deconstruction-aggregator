# Combating Cult Mind Control

Steve Hassan's *Combating Cult Mind Control* is essential reading for understanding the psychological mechanics of coercive control. The book provides a framework for identifying undue influence, analyzing how groups maintain member compliance, and supporting people in recovery. While covering multiple groups, Hassan includes substantial analysis of Scientology's specific tactics.

The book combines psychological theory with practical guidance, making it valuable both for academic understanding and for families and individuals affected by high-control groups. Hassan's work is informed by his personal experience exiting the Unification Church and his subsequent career in cult recovery and education.
