# Scientology Money Project

The Scientology Money Project, maintained by Jefferson Hawkins and others, investigates the organization's financial practices in detail. The site documents fundraising strategies, the escalating costs of the Bridge to Total Freedom (reaching $300,000-500,000+), mandatory donation schemes, and the financial impact on individuals and families. Extensive research shows how the organization systematizes financial extraction.

The project includes case studies of specific financial practices, analysis of organizational income streams, and first-hand accounts from people who experienced financial pressure within Scientology. It's an essential resource for understanding economic exploitation as a key mechanism of organizational control.
