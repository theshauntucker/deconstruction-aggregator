# Scientology and the Aftermath

*Scientology and the Aftermath* is a multi-season investigative series produced by Emmy-winning actress Leah Remini and former Church executive Mike Rinder. The show systematically investigates specific abuses and cases of disconnection, featuring victims and family members separated from loved ones. Each episode explores a distinct aspect of Scientology's harm—from coercive labor practices to financial predation to harassment.

The series is particularly valuable for its human-centered approach, documenting the real-world emotional and psychological impact of Scientology's policies. The combination of professional investigation with intimate testimony makes it highly effective for understanding the organization's mechanisms of control and the paths people take to escape.
