# Headley v. Church of Scientology

Marc Headley's federal lawsuit alleges that Scientology violated labor laws through forced labor practices in the Sea Org and at the International Base in Hemet, California. The lawsuit documents systematic wage theft, coerced work, and conditions analogous to human trafficking. Though ultimately settled, the lawsuit generated substantial court documentation of Sea Org labor practices.

Court records and pleadings are available through PACER and legal databases like Justia. This case provides critical legal documentation of the Sea Org's labor structure and violations, serving as an important complement to personal narratives and journalistic investigation.
