# The Unbreakable Miss Lovely

Tony Ortega's *The Unbreakable Miss Lovely* tells the story of journalist Paulette Cooper and Scientology's documented campaign to destroy her—Operation Freakout. The organization deployed Fair Game tactics including frame-ups, harassment, infiltration, and psychological operations. Cooper's case is one of the most thoroughly documented examples of Scientology's retaliatory practices against critics.

The book demonstrates the Fair Game doctrine in action with contemporary documentation and court records. It's a crucial case study in how high-control organizations respond to criticism and the long-term impacts on targeted individuals. Cooper's survival and ultimate vindication provides a historical context for understanding current Fair Game tactics.
