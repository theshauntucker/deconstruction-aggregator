# The Aftermath Foundation

The Aftermath Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by Leah Remini that provides direct support to people leaving Scientology. The foundation offers financial assistance, exit counseling, housing support, and legal aid to defectors and their families. It serves as both a practical resource and a symbol of organized community support for Scientology survivors.

The foundation's website documents its programs and impact, including testimonials from people it has assisted. It represents one of the few formal institutional responses to Scientology's documented harms and provides a crucial safety net for people in the vulnerable early stages of leaving the organization.
