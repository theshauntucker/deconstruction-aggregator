# Going Clear: Scientology and the Prison of Belief (HBO Documentary)

Director Alex Gibney's HBO adaptation of Lawrence Wright's investigation brings powerful visual storytelling to Scientology's practices. The documentary features extensive interviews with high-level defectors and provides unprecedented access to firsthand accounts of the organization's isolation tactics, forced disconnection, and harassment of critics.

The film is particularly effective in demonstrating the emotional and psychological impact of Scientology's control mechanisms. It won a Primetime Emmy for Outstanding Investigative Journalism and remains one of the most widely viewed critical examinations of the organization.
