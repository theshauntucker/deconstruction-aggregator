# The Underground Bunker

Journalist Tony Ortega's *Underground Bunker* is the most consistent and current source for Scientology news. Ortega, a former religion reporter, updates daily with litigation developments, organizational reporting, interviews with defectors, and analysis of the organization's activities. The blog serves as an informal hub for the ex-Scientology community and frequently breaks news before mainstream media coverage.

The site's extensive archive and cross-referenced articles make it invaluable for tracking Scientology's evolution, legal challenges, and the experiences of people leaving the organization. Ortega's reporting combines investigative journalism with accessibility, making complex legal and organizational issues understandable for general audiences.
