# Operation Clambake (xenu.net)

Operation Clambake, maintained by Arnie Lerma since 1996, is the oldest continuously-running critical resource on Scientology. The site functions as a comprehensive encyclopedia housing internal Scientology documents, court filings, historical research, and analysis. Its longevity and extensive archive make it invaluable for tracking the organization's evolution.

The site includes rare documents obtained through litigation, detailed timelines of major events, and reference materials on Scientology's foundational teachings and organizational structure. While the site's design is dated, its informational depth remains unmatched for historical research and document preservation.
