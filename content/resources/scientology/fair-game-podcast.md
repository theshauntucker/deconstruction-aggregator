# Fair Game Podcast

The Fair Game Podcast is a continuation of the investigative work featured in *Scientology and the Aftermath*. Leah Remini and Mike Rinder conduct extended interviews with defectors, victims, and family members, allowing for more nuanced exploration of specific cases and experiences. The longer format enables deeper investigation of complex organizational dynamics.

The podcast is available on major podcast platforms and archives extensive interviews that serve as primary source material for understanding Scientology's impact on individuals and families. Episode topics range from specific disconnection cases to detailed analysis of Fair Game tactics to accounts of escape and recovery.
