# Mike Rinder's Blog (Archive)

Mike Rinder served as the head of Scientology's Office of Special Affairs (OSA)—the organization's intelligence and legal defense arm—before leaving the church in 2007. His blog documented his experience inside the organization and provided detailed analysis of specific cases and organizational policies. Rinder passed away in January 2025, but his archived blog remains an invaluable primary source from someone with deep insider knowledge.

The blog is particularly valuable for understanding how Scientology's Fair Game doctrine operates in practice, how the organization orchestrates legal strategy, and the psychological mechanisms used to enforce loyalty. Rinder's posts include extensive engagement with current events and ongoing litigation involving the church.
