# Lisa McPherson Case Filings

Lisa McPherson was a Scientology member who died in 1995 while under the organization's care during an isolation treatment called the "Introspection Rundown." Her family filed a wrongful death lawsuit; court documents detail how Scientology staff withheld medical care, isolated McPherson, and failed to report her death immediately. The case became a landmark litigation demonstrating documented negligence and the dangers of the Introspection Rundown.

Court filings are available through Florida's court system. The case provides crucial documentation of specific organizational practices and their consequences, making it essential primary source material for understanding Scientology's documented harms.
