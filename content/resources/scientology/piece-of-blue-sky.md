# A Piece of Blue Sky: Scientology, Dianetics and L. Ron Hubbard Exposed

Jon Atack's *A Piece of Blue Sky* is a thorough historical investigation combining biographical research on L. Ron Hubbard with organizational analysis. Atack spent years inside Scientology and conducted extensive research after leaving, producing one of the most detailed accounts of Hubbard's fabricated autobiography and the early evolution of Scientology's belief system and control mechanisms.

The book is densely researched with extensive documentation of sources, making it invaluable for understanding Scientology's historical development. Atack traces how Dianetics evolved into Scientology, how the organization's financial and control mechanisms developed, and how Hubbard's leadership established patterns that persist under current leader David Miscavige.
