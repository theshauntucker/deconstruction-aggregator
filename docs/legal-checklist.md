# Legal Checklist & Risk Management

## Formation & Jurisdiction

### Entity Structure
- **Wyoming Anonymous LLC** (preferred)
  - Owner names NOT on articles of incorporation
  - No state income tax
  - Strong charging-order protections (rare benefit)
  - Use registered agent ($50-100/year)
  - FinCEN BOI reporting required but NOT public

### Additional Protection
- **Register as foreign LLC in CA or TX** (optional but recommended)
  - Provides anti-SLAPP protections
  - 40 states + DC have anti-SLAPP laws
  - Covers legal defense costs and damages if frivolous lawsuit filed

## Copyright & DMCA Compliance

### Required Legal Documents
1. **DMCA Designated Agent Registration** ($6 with US Copyright Office)
   - Register before hosting any user-generated content
   - Include visible DMCA policy on site
   - Provide takedown and counter-notice process

2. **Fair Use Four-Factor Test**
   - Purpose & character: Is content transformative?
   - Nature of work: Is it factual, creative, or published?
   - Amount used: Minimal copying vs. wholesale reproduction?
   - Effect on market: Does it harm the original's economic value?

### Key Precedent Cases

#### Watch Tower Bible v. "Kevin McFree" (Fair Use FOUND)
- Commentary on Watchtower materials = protected fair use
- Verdict: Fair use can apply to religious criticism

#### RTC v. Lerma (Fair Use NOT FOUND)
- Posting entire Scientology OT materials WITHOUT commentary = NOT fair use
- **LESSON**: Always add transformative commentary. Never host full docs without analysis.

## Litigation Risk Assessment by Organization

### Scientology (HIGH RISK, MANAGEABLE)
**Aggression level**: Most litigation-happy religious organization on record
- Filed thousands of DMCA takedowns
- Sued Time Warner for $416M
- LRH quote on strategy: "The purpose of the suit is to harass"

**Mitigations**:
- Media liability insurance ($78/month, $936/year)
- Anonymous LLC structure
- Strict fair use discipline
- Don't host OT materials directly
- Register in anti-SLAPP jurisdiction

### Watch Tower/Jehovah's Witnesses (MODERATE RISK)
**Aggression level**: Moderate but persistent
- Filed 72+ DMCA 512(h) subpoenas since 2017 to unmask critics
- Strategy: identify critics, then pursue offline
- EFF has defended critics successfully

**Main risk**: Identity exposure via subpoena
**Mitigation**: Anonymous LLC + legal fund awareness

### LDS/Intellectual Reserve Inc. (LOW-MODERATE RISK)
**Aggression level**: Low to moderate, focused on IP
- Enforces copyrights on modern materials
- Sued UTLM (Utah Light the Truth) in 1999
- Protects trademarks: "Mormon", "LDS", "CTR"
- Won't sue over CES Letter (CC-licensed)

**Main risk**: Trademark/copyright takedowns
**Mitigation**: Use proper attribution, avoid trademark use as descriptors

### All Other Organizations (LOW RISK)
- Generally not litigious
- Prefer ignoring critics to lawsuits
- Standard defamation/copyright protocols apply

## Defamation Risk

### "Cult" is Opinion Speech
- Generally protected in US courts
- Supported by BITE Model framework
- Must present as opinion, not fact

### International Risk
- **2025 Italian ruling**: Found "cult" defamatory for Soka Gakkai
- Risk: Hosting criticism accessible in EU could trigger GDPR complications
- Mitigation: Geo-block Italy if necessary, frame content as opinion + disclaimer

### Best Practice Phrasing
"While [organization] is recognized as a cult by [credentialed source], opinions vary. Here's the BITE Model analysis..."

## Required Legal Pages

1. **Terms of Service**
   - User conduct rules
   - Content moderation
   - Liability disclaimers
   - IP policy

2. **Privacy Policy**
   - GDPR compliant
   - CCPA compliant
   - Analytics disclosure
   - Email capture terms

3. **Content Disclaimer**
   - Educational purpose statement
   - Opinion vs. fact separation
   - Not professional advice

4. **DMCA Policy Page**
   - Takedown procedure
   - Counter-notice procedure
   - Agent contact info

5. **Cookie Consent Banner**
   - EU/UK requirement
   - Required before analytics

6. **Affiliate Disclosure**
   - FTC requirement
   - Clear for all monetized links
   - Format: "We earn a commission if you click and purchase"

## Section 230 Protection

### What Section 230 Protects
- User-generated forum posts
- User-submitted comments
- User-created reviews
- UGC moderation decisions

### What Section 230 Does NOT Protect
- Content you authored
- AI-generated content (untested)
- Republished third-party content without fair use protection
- Monetized user content (gray area)

**Implication**: Forum moderator work is protected. Your own blog posts are not.

## 7 Categories of Legally Hostable Content

### 1. CES Letter
- License: **CC BY-NC-ND 3.0** (Creative Commons)
- Source: official ceesletter.org
- Hosting requirement: Attribution + link to original

### 2. WikiIslam Content
- License: **CC BY-NC 3.0**
- Source: wikiislam.net
- Hosting requirement: Attribution

### 3. Federal & State Court Filings
- License: **Public Domain** (US gov works)
- Filings in Scientology cases, JW cases, etc.
- Source: PACER.gov, court websites

### 4. Australian Royal Commission Reports
- License: **Public Domain** (Australian gov docs)
- Child Abuse Royal Commission includes religious org findings
- Source: rcircsa.org.au

### 5. FBI FOIA Documents
- License: **Public Domain** (US gov works)
- FBI files on Scientology, cults, etc.
- Source: vault.fbi.gov

### 6. Pre-1929 Historical Texts
- License: **Public Domain** (US copyright expires 95 years after publication)
- Religious texts, historical church docs
- Source: archive.org, Internet Archive

### 7. BITE Model PDF
- License: **Free download, trademark attribution required**
- Creator: Dr. Steven Hassan
- Usage: Host/link with author credit
- Source: liftonmodel.com, Steven Hassan's site

## Red Flags to Avoid

- **Don't host full OT documents** (Scientology) without detailed commentary
- **Don't use trademark terms as primary descriptors** (avoid "The Mormon Site")
- **Don't claim professional medical authority** (use disclaimers)
- **Don't republish entire news articles** (excerpt + link instead)
- **Don't impersonate sources** (be clear who wrote what)
- **Don't leave DMCA notices unresponsed** (respond within 30 days)
