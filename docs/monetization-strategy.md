# Monetization Strategy

## Revenue Stack Architecture

Therapy affiliate commissions are the **PRIMARY revenue driver**, not ads alone. One BetterHelp conversion ($100-200) equals 10K-20K ad pageview equivalents.

---

## Phase 1: Bootstrap (0-10K PV, Months 1-12)

**Target Monthly Revenue**: $200-800

### Ad Networks
- **AdSense** ($2-5 RPM)
  - Allows educational religious criticism (NOT hate speech)
  - Frame site as recovery/wellness/education
  - Easiest approval
  - Lower RPM but reliable

### Affiliates
- **Amazon Associates** (4.5% commission)
  - Books: deconstruction guides, psychology of religion
  - Workbooks: journaling, self-help
  - Link from resource pages

- **BetterHelp** (Impact Radius)
  - $100-200 per customer conversion
  - Primary revenue focus
  - Place CTAs throughout site
  - "Find a therapist" links

- **Talkspace** (FlexOffers)
  - Per-lead payouts
  - Text-based therapy option
  - Lower conversion than BetterHelp but complement

### Direct
- **Ko-fi donations**
  - Install Ko-fi button
  - One-time or recurring
  - No platform fee
  - Appeals to passionate community members

### Content Monetization
- **Google Adsense enabled** on all pages
- **Amazon book links** on resource pages
- **Affiliate disclosure above fold** on every monetized page

---

## Phase 2: Growth (10K-25K PV, Months 12-18)

**Target Monthly Revenue**: $1,500-3,000

### Ad Networks (Upgraded)
- **Monumetric** ($8-15 RPM)
  - Requires 10K monthly pageviews
  - Blocks religious-themed ads by default but accepts religious-content sites
  - Frame as recovery/wellness content
  - 3-5x better RPM than AdSense

### Expanded Affiliates
- **Online-Therapy.com**
  - 50% commission (best in industry)
  - 90-day cookie = repeat conversions count
  - $50-100 per sign-up
  - Emphasize video therapy option

- **Coach/Therapist Sponsorships**
  - Direct outreach to therapy practices
  - $100-300/month per sponsor
  - "Featured therapist" section
  - Goal: 3-5 sponsors = $300-1,500/mo

### Community Revenue
- **Patreon launch**
  - $3, $10, $25 tier structure
  - Exclusive content: interview transcripts, deep-dives
  - Community Discord for patrons
  - Realistic: 50-100 patrons at average $10 = $500-1,000/mo

### Content Monetization
- All Phase 1 streams continue
- Patreon for exclusive content
- Sponsorship opportunities created

---

## Phase 3: Scale (25K-100K PV, Months 18-30)

**Target Monthly Revenue**: $5,000-8,000

### Premium Ad Networks
- **Raptive** (formerly AdThrive)
  - Requires 25K monthly pageviews
  - $15-22 RPM (2-3x Monumetric)
  - Approval process: 3-4 weeks
  - Best for affiliate-heavy sites

- **Mediavine Journey**
  - Alternative: 1K monthly sessions entry (lower PV threshold)
  - $14-25 RPM
  - More strict content moderation but slightly better RPM

### Digital Products
- **Workbooks & Guides**
  - "Deconstruction Workbook" ($10-15)
  - "BITE Model Analysis for [Religion]" ($5-10 each)
  - Sold via Gumroad or SendOwl
  - Realistic: 20-50 sales/month = $200-750/mo

- **Video Courses**
  - "Religious Trauma Recovery" course ($49-99)
  - YouTube + Teachable/Kajabi
  - 5-10 sales/month = $250-990/mo

### Substack Paid Newsletter
- Migrate email list to Substack
- Free tier: Weekly roundup + top resources
- Paid tier: $5/month
  - Exclusive interviews with therapists
  - Deep-dive analyses
  - Ad-free version
- Realistic goal: 500 paid subs x $5 = $2,500/mo

### Direct Ad Sales to Therapists
- Reach out to therapy platforms, online counseling services
- Custom ad spots in sidebar + newsletter
- $200-500/month per sponsor
- Goal: 3-5 direct sponsors = $600-2,500/mo

### Affiliate Networks
- All Phase 2 streams continue
- Increase therapist sponsorships to 10+ at higher rates
- Launch affiliate program for coaches

---

## Phase 4: Mature (100K+ PV, Months 30+)

**Target Monthly Revenue**: $8,000-18,000

### Full Stack Active
- Raptive/Mediavine: $2,000-4,000/mo
- BetterHelp + Online-Therapy: $2,000-3,000/mo
- Therapist sponsorships: $1,500-2,500/mo
- Substack paid: $1,000-2,500/mo
- Digital products: $500-1,000/mo
- Patreon: $500-1,000/mo
- Ko-fi + direct donations: $200-500/mo

### Explore 501(c)(3) Status
- Google Ad Grants ($10K/month free Google Ads)
- Tax-deductible donations
- Foundation grants
- Models: Recovery for Religion, CES Letter Foundation both operate as 501(c)(3)s

### Alternative: Acquisition
- At 100K+ PV and $8-10K/mo revenue, site becomes acquisition target
- Bolt-on to larger media company
- Exit value: $200K-500K+ depending on profitability

## Ad Network Policy Notes

### AdSense
- Allows educational religious criticism
- Does NOT allow hate speech
- Frame site as: recovery, wellness, education
- Avoid: accusatory language, slurs, political rants

### Monumetric
- Blocks religious-themed ads by default
- Whitelist request: "Site covers religious deconstruction and recovery"
- Approval rate: ~70% of requests
- Response time: 1-2 weeks

### Raptive / Mediavine
- Follow Google's content policies (YMYL)
- E-E-A-T requirement: credentialed contributors
- NO restrictions on religious criticism per se
- Approval more about traffic quality than content ideology

### Ezoic
- Raised threshold to 250K MAU in Feb 2026
- No longer entry-level option
- Consider for mature sites only

## Affiliate Program Setup

### Impact Radius (BetterHelp)
- Registration: impactradius.com
- Payout: Monthly ACH
- Commission: $100-200 per customer
- Cookie: 30-90 days
- Tracking: Links to BetterHelp signup
- Placement: Every therapy mention + sidebar CTA

### FlexOffers (Talkspace & Others)
- Registration: flexoffers.com
- Payout: Monthly ACH or PayPal
- Variety: 50+ therapy/wellness brands
- Commission: Varies by program
- Tracking: Robust link generation

### Amazon Associates
- Registration: amazon.com/associates
- Payout: Monthly to linked account
- Commission: 4.5% (books, low-ticket items)
- Cookie: 24 hours
- Compliance: Must disclose

### Direct Sponsorships
- Email outreach to therapy practices
- Custom landing page per sponsor
- Contract: $100-500/month, 3-12 months
- Deliverable: Logo in sidebar, newsletter mention, dedicated page

## FTC Disclosure Strategy

### Required Format
Place at **top of every monetized page**:

```
This page contains affiliate links. We earn a commission if you click and purchase 
through these links. This does not affect the price you pay.
```

### Substack Disclosure
```
Paid posts are marked [PAID SUBSCRIBER ONLY]. Free subscribers get weekly roundup; 
paid subscribers get interviews and deep-dives.
```

### Therapy Affiliate Disclosure
```
We recommend BetterHelp. We earn $100-200 per customer referral. 
We only recommend therapists because we believe they help; our commission doesn't 
influence our recommendations.
```

## Revenue Acceleration Tactics

### Quick Wins (Month 1-6)
- Launch therapy CTAs immediately
- Join affiliate networks before building traffic
- Set up Ko-fi for early supporters
- Email list capture with lead magnet

### Mid-Term (Month 6-18)
- Apply for Monumetric at 10K PV
- Launch Patreon
- Reach out to 10 therapists for sponsorships
- Create first digital product (workbook)

### Long-Term (Month 18+)
- Apply for Raptive at 25K PV
- Scale Substack paid subscriber base
- Explore 501(c)(3) conversion
- Consider content syndication deals

---

## Key Principle

**Therapy affiliates >> ad revenue for first 18 months.** Don't optimize for ad RPM at the expense of therapist CTAs and affiliate placement. One converted BetterHelp customer pays more than 20K ad impressions.
